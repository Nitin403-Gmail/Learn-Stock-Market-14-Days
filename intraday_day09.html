üåä Riding the Wave with Institutions

ORB (Day 8) is for catching the morning explosion. But what if you miss it? Or what if the market is trending steadily?

That's where the VWAP Pullback Strategy comes in. We know Institutions love to buy near the average price (VWAP). So, we wait for the price to come to us.

‚öôÔ∏è The Setup Rules

  • 1
    Identify the Trend Price must be trading CLEARLY above VWAP for a Buy trend (or clearly below for Sell).
  • 2
    The Pullback (The Dip) Wait for the price to come back down and touch (or get very close to) the VWAP line. Do not buy yet.
  • 3
    The Confirmation Watch for a GREEN candle to form at the VWAP line. This proves buyers have stepped in to defend the average price.
  • 4
    The Execution Buy above the high of that Green candle.
    Stop Loss: Just below the VWAP line. (Very small risk!).
VWAP Line

üß† Why this strategy works?

1. Value Buying

Imagine seeing an iPhone at $1000, then it goes to $1200, then drops back to $1050. Smart buyers will buy at $1050 thinking it's a "good deal" relative to the trend.

2. Tight Stop Loss

This is the biggest advantage. Since you are buying right at the support line (VWAP), your stop loss is tiny. If the line breaks, you get out immediately with a small scratch.

‚úÖ Today's Action Steps

  1. Open your charting platform. Add the VWAP indicator.
  2. Look at a trending stock (e.g., usually IT or Bank stocks on a trend day).
  3. Spot how many times it touched the VWAP line and bounced back up.
  4. This strategy requires Patience. You cannot chase price; you must wait for it to come to you.
