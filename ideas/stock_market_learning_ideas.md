# Stock Market Learning Ideas

## 1. Core Concepts & Fundamentals

- **Financial Literacy 101**: Understanding assets, liabilities, equity, and simple compounding.
- **Macroeconomics**: How interest rates, inflation, and GDP affect the market.
- **Sector Analysis**: identifying cyclic vs. defensive sectors (e.g., FMCG vs. Metals).
- **IPO Analysis**: How to read a DRHP (Draft Red Herring Prospectus) and evaluate new listings.

## 2. Technical Analysis Mastery

- **Price Action Deep Dive**: Moving beyond indicators to understand pure candlestick psychology.
- **Volume Spread Analysis (VSA)**: Understanding the relationship between price movement and volume.
- **Multi-Timeframe Analysis**: correlating trends across Monthly, Weekly, Daily, and Intraday charts.
- **Pattern Recognition**: Building a personal "playbook" of high-probability chart patterns (e.g., Cup & Handle, Head & Shoulders).

## 3. Trading Styles Exploration

- **Intraday Trading**:
  - Momentum strategies (Gap up/down).
  - Mean reversion strategies.
  - Understanding Open Range Breakout (ORB).
- **Swing Trading**: Capturing trends over days or weeks using weekly pivots and moving averages.
- **Options Trading**:
  - Understanding "The Greeks" (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega).
  - Hedging strategies (Iron Condors, Straddles) for neutral markets.
  - Directional option buying vs. selling.

## 4. Risk Management & Psychology

- **Position Sizing**: The mathematical approach to never losing more than 1-2% of capital per trade.
- **Trading Journal**: Creating a rigorous habit of documenting every entry, exit, and emotion.
- **The "Checklist" System**: standardized pre-trade routine to eliminate impulsive decisions.
- **Biases**: Studying behavioral finance (Confirmation Bias, FOMO, Sunk Cost Fallacy).

## 5. Practical Application & Tools

- **Paper Trading**: Running a simulated portfolio for 3 months before deploying real capital.
- **Backtesting**: Using code (Python/PineScript) or manual methods to verify strategy performance over the last 5 years.
- **Screening Setup**: Configuring tools (like TradingView or Chartink) to auto-detect potential setups.
- **News Filtration**: Learning to distinguish between "noise" and market-moving news.

## 6. Project-Based Learning Ideas

- **"The 14-Day Challenge"**: Complete the existing course in the repo and backtest one strategy from it.
- **Sector Rotation Model**: Build a simple spreadsheet tracking sector performance week-over-week.
- **Algorithm Design**: Write a simple pseudo-code for a trading bot based on a specific logic.
