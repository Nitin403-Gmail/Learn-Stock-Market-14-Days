üìä What Is Price Action?

Price action is the study of price movement itself‚Äîwithout relying on indicators. It's watching what buyers and sellers are actually doing, not what some lagging indicator tells you they did.

"Price is king. Everything else is just noise." ‚Äî Professional Trading Principle

Why Price Action Matters Most

  • It's leading, not lagging - Indicators calculate from past prices; price action shows you NOW
  • It works on any market - Stocks, forex, crypto‚Äîprice behavior is universal
  • It works on any timeframe - From 1-minute charts to monthly charts
  • It's what professional traders use - Institutions don't need 10 indicators

üí° The Simple Truth

Every indicator you've ever seen‚ÄîRSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands‚Äîis just a mathematical manipulation of price. Why study the derivative when you can study the source? Master price action first. Add indicators later (if ever).

üìà The 3 Market States

At any moment, a market is in one of three states. Your job is to identify which one, and trade accordingly.

1. Uptrend (Bullish)

Defined by a series of Higher Highs (HH) and Higher Lows (HL).

üí° Critical Rule

Trade WITH the trend, not against it. Fighting the trend is the #1 reason beginners lose money. If the market is going up, look for buying opportunities. If it's going down, look for selling opportunities. If it's sideways, wait or trade the range.

üîÑ Identifying Trend Changes

Trends don't last forever. Learning to spot when a trend is weakening or reversing is crucial.

Signs of an Uptrend Weakening

  1. Lower High appears - Price fails to make a new high
  2. Equal highs - Price hits resistance and stalls multiple times
  3. Break of previous low - The structure starts breaking down

Signs of a Downtrend Weakening

  1. Higher Low appears - Price fails to make a new low
  2. Equal lows - Price hits support and bounces multiple times
  3. Break of previous high - The structure starts changing

üí° The Break of Structure (BOS)

When price breaks above the most recent lower high in a downtrend, or below the most recent higher low in an uptrend, it signals a potential change of character. This is often your first sign that the trend may be reversing.

The Change of Character (CHoCH)

A Change of Character occurs when:

  • In an uptrend: Price breaks below the most recent Higher Low
  • In a downtrend: Price breaks above the most recent Lower High

This is your alert to start looking for trades in the NEW direction.

üìù Action Step 1: Chart Analysis Practice (20 minutes)

Open TradingView and analyze 5 different stocks:

  1. Identify: Is it in an uptrend, downtrend, or range?
  2. Mark the Higher Highs/Higher Lows OR Lower Highs/Lower Lows
  3. Write down your analysis for each

Suggested stocks: AAPL, MSFT, TSLA, SPY, NVDA

üìù Action Step 2: Historical Trend Study (15 minutes)

Pick one stock (like SPY - S&P 500 ETF) and zoom out to the daily chart:

  1. Find the last major uptrend‚Äîhow many months did it last?
  2. Find the last major downtrend‚Äîwhat caused it to reverse?
  3. Identify 2-3 range/consolidation periods

üìù Action Step 3: Trend Change Practice (10 minutes)

On the same charts, find examples of:

  1. A trend that reversed (uptrend to downtrend or vice versa)
  2. Mark where the first sign of weakness appeared
  3. Mark where the Change of Character occurred

üîë Key Takeaways

  1. Price action is the foundation of all technical analysis‚Äîmaster this first
  2. Markets exist in 3 states: uptrend (HH/HL), downtrend (LH/LL), or range
  3. Always trade with the trend, not against it
  4. Watch for Higher Lows in downtrends and Lower Highs in uptrends as early reversal signs
  5. A Break of Structure (BOS) signals potential trend change
