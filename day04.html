What Each Part Tells You

  • Body Size: Large body = strong conviction. Small body = indecision.
  • Upper Wick: Sellers rejected higher prices. Long upper wick = selling pressure.
  • Lower Wick: Buyers rejected lower prices. Long lower wick = buying pressure.
  • Color: Green/White = Closed higher than open (bullish). Red/Black = Closed lower than open (bearish).

Patterns Only Work When:

  1. At Key Levels: Support, resistance, round numbers, moving averages
