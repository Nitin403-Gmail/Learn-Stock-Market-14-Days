üîé Shapes Are Just Psychology

Chart patterns are not magic drawings. They represent the collective psychology of millions of traders. When you see a "Triangle," it's not geometry; it's a coil of energy waiting to explode. We trade the Explosion (Breakout).

üí° Mentor's Perspective

1. Continuation Patterns (Trend Resuming)

These form when the market takes a breather before continuing its original direction. These are the safest to trade.

2. Reversal Patterns (Trend Changing)

These signal that the current trend is dying and a new one is starting. Risky but high reward.

üõë The Anatomy of a Valid Breakout

Many beginners lose money on "False Breakouts". How to filter them?

  • Volume Confirmation: The breakout candle MUST have high volume. Trends need fuel.
  • Closing Rule: Wait for the candle to CLOSE outside the pattern. Do not jump in while the candle is still moving.
  • The Retest (Safest): Waiting for price to break, come back to touch the line, and then bounce is the safest entry.
