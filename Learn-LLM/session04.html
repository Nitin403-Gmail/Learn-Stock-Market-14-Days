What is Prompting?

üí¨ Definition

Prompting is the art of crafting instructions that help AI models produce the responses you want. Think of it as giving clear directions to a skilled assistant.

The quality of your prompt directly determines the quality of the AI's response. A well-crafted prompt can turn a mediocre AI response into an excellent one.

Good Prompt Characteristics
  • Specific: Clear and detailed instructions
  • Contextual: Provides relevant background
  • Structured: Organized and easy to follow
  • Actionable: Tells AI exactly what to do
Common Prompting Mistakes
  • Too Vague: "Write something" vs "Write a 500-word article"
  • No Context: Missing background information
  • Overly Complex: Confusing multi-part requests
  • Unclear Goals: What success looks like

Basic Prompting Techniques

1. Be Specific

Instead of "Write a story," say "Write a 800-word mystery story about a detective solving a jewelry theft in Victorian London."

2. Provide Context

Give the AI background information it needs: "As a marketing expert with 10 years experience..." or "Using React and TypeScript..."

3. Specify Format

Tell the AI how to structure the response: "Format your answer as a bullet-point list" or "Use markdown formatting"

4. Define Output Length

Set expectations: "Write a 500-word article" or "Provide a brief 2-3 sentence summary"

5. Use Examples

Show what you want: "Like this example: [example here]" or "Similar to how [famous person/company] does it"

6. Iterate and Refine

If the first response isn't perfect, provide feedback: "Make it more formal" or "Add more technical details"

Project: Personal AI Assistant

‚è∞ Time Estimate: 45-60 minutes
üéØ Objective: Create a comprehensive prompt that builds a personal AI assistant for a specific use case
Project Overview

You'll create a detailed prompt that instructs an AI to act as a personal assistant for a specific scenario. This project will teach you how to craft complex, multi-part prompts that produce useful, actionable results.

Project Steps
Step 1: Choose your scenario (10 minutes)
Pick one: Fitness Coach, Study Buddy, Meal Planner, Budget Advisor, or Career Counselor
Step 2: Define requirements (10 minutes)
What should your AI assistant be able to do? List 5-7 specific capabilities
Step 3: Craft your master prompt (15 minutes)
Write a detailed prompt that includes role, capabilities, constraints, and examples
Step 4: Test and refine (15-20 minutes)
Test your prompt with LM Studio, iterate based on results, document improvements
Step 5: Create documentation (10 minutes)
Write a brief guide explaining how to use your AI assistant prompt
Example Scenario: Fitness Coach

Sample Prompt Structure:

"You are an experienced personal fitness coach with 15 years of experience helping people achieve their fitness goals. You have certifications in personal training, nutrition, and sports psychology."

"Your role is to create personalized workout and nutrition plans, provide motivation, track progress, and answer fitness-related questions."

"Always prioritize safety, encourage sustainable habits, and be encouraging while being realistic about goals."

üí° Pro Tips:
  • Start simple, then add complexity as you test
  • Use your first few conversations to understand what works
  • Keep iterating - each test improves your prompt
  • Document what changes improve results

Advanced Prompting Tips

Temperature Settings
  • 0.1-0.3: Factual, consistent responses
  • 0.4-0.6: Balanced creativity and accuracy
  • 0.7-1.0: Highly creative, varied responses
Context Management

Keep conversations focused by periodically restating context or using shorter conversation threads for complex tasks.

Prompt Templates
Template Structure:
1. Define the role/expertise
2. Specify the task/goal
3. Provide constraints/limitations
4. Give examples if needed
5. Define output format
Common Patterns
  • "Act as a [role] and [task]"
  • "You are an expert [field]"
  • "Explain [topic] as if to [audience]"
  • "Create [output] following [format]"
