Amateurs wake up at 9:10 AM and start trading. Professionals have a routine. Intraday trading is a high-performance sport. You cannot just show up; you must prepare.

üìÖ The Daily Schedule

08:30 AM - The Warm Up
  • Read Global Market cues (US Market Close, Asian Markets).
  • Check SGX Nifty (GIFT Nifty) for Gap Up/Gap Down indication.
  • Shortlist 4-5 stocks based on News or Daily Charts.
09:00 AM - Pre-Market Analysis
  • Market Opens for Pre-Market session. Check actual Open prices.
  • Adjust your Support/Resistance levels on the chart.
  • Mental Check: "Am I calm? Am I ready to accept risk?"
09:15 AM to 10:30 AM - The Kill Zone
  • Market Opens. Volatility is high.
  • Look for ORB Setups (Day 8) or Momentum trades.
  • Most pros make their money here and close the shop.
12:30 PM to 01:30 PM - The European Open
  • European markets open. Volatility spikes again.
  • Look for VWAP Pullbacks (Day 9) or Reversals.
03:30 PM - Post Market Review
  • Market Closes. Journaling Time (Critical).
  • Screenshot your trades. Write down mistakes.
  • Close the laptop and forget the market.
