üìà What is Intraday Trading?

Intraday trading (or Day Trading) means buying and selling stocks within the same trading day.

  • No Overnight Risk: You close all positions before the market closes (3:30 PM IST). You sleep peacefully without worrying about global news affecting your portfolio overnight.
  • Leverage: Brokers often provide leverage (e.g., 5x), meaning with ‚Çπ10,000 capital, you can trade stocks worth ‚Çπ50,000. (Note: Use leverage wisely!)
  • Daily Income Potential: You don't wait years for compounding; you realize profits (or losses) every single day.

üìú The 3 Golden Rules of Intraday

Write these down. Stick them on your wall. Memorize them.

Rule #1: Protect Your Capital

Your capital is your inventory. If you lose your capital, you are out of business. Never risk more than 1-2% of your total capital on a single trade.

Rule #2: Plan the Trade, Trade the Plan

Never enter a trade without knowing your Entry, Stop Loss, and Target beforehand. If you are thinking during the trade, you are already losing.

Rule #3: Acceptance

Accept that losses are part of the game. A loss is just the cost of doing business. If your stop loss hits, exit. Do not hope. Hope is not a strategy.
