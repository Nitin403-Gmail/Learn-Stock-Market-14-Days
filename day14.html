üìã The 7 Pillars: Complete Summary

These are the 20% of concepts that create 80% of trading success. Master these deeply.

Pillar 1: Market Structure & Price Action

Read price movement directly. Identify uptrends (HH/HL), downtrends (LH/LL), and ranges. Trade with the trend, never against it. Break of Structure signals potential trend changes.

Pillar 2: Support & Resistance

The foundation of all trading decisions. Know where to enter, exit, and place stops. Role reversal creates the highest probability trades. Trade bounces in ranges, breakouts in trends.

Pillar 3: Risk Management

The #1 skill for survival. Never risk more than 1-2% per trade. Position Size = Risk √∑ (Entry - Stop). Only take 1:2 or better risk-reward setups. This is non-negotiable.

Pillar 4: Candlestick Patterns

5 patterns that work: Pin Bar, Engulfing, Inside Bar, Doji, Morning/Evening Star. Context is everything‚Äîpatterns only work at key levels with confirmation.

Pillar 5: Moving Averages

The only indicator you need. 20/50/200 EMAs define trend direction and act as dynamic support/resistance. Trade pullbacks to MAs in trending markets.

Pillar 6: Trading Psychology

80-90% of success is mental. Master the 5 emotional traps: Fear, Greed, FOMO, Revenge Trading, Overconfidence. Think in probabilities, focus on process over outcome.

Pillar 7: Trading Plan & Execution

A written plan removes emotion. 7 components: Market, Style, Entry, Exit, Risk, Routine, Rules. Journal every trade. Review weekly. Improve 1% at a time.
