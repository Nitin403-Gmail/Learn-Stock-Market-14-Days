üß† Trading is 80% Psychology

You can have the best strategy in the world (like the ones we learned in Days 8-10), but if you cannot control your emotions, you will lose. The market is designed to trigger your deepest fears and desires.

üëπ The 3 Demons of Trading

üò® FEAR

Symptoms: Exiting a winning trade too early because you are scared profits will vanish. Or refusing to enter a perfect setup because you are scared to lose.

Cure: Trust your Backtesting. Trust your Stop Loss. Accepting the risk before entering eliminates fear.

ü§ë GREED

Symptoms: Holding a trade passed your target hoping for "more". Adding to a losing position hoping it will come back.

Cure: Have a fixed Target. Once hit, book it (or at least book 80%). Nobody went broke booking profits.

üèÉ FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)

Symptoms: Seeing a big green candle and jumping in blindly because "everyone is making money". This usually happens right at the top.

Cure: "There is always another bus." The market will be here tomorrow. Missing a trade is better than losing money on a bad trade.

üßò The Trader's Mindset: Probability thinking

Amateur traders think in terms of "Right vs Wrong". Professionals think in terms of "Probabilities".

"I don't need to know what is going to happen next to make money. I just need to know that over a series of 20 trades, my edge (strategy) will make me profitable."
