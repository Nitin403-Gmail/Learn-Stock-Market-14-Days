‚ö° Speed is Everything

In intraday trading, seconds matter. A 5-second delay in loading a chart or placing an order can turn a profit into a loss. Today, we configure your battle station to be lean, mean, and fast.

We are not here to look at pretty charts; we are here to extract money from the market efficiently.

üìä 1. Analysis Setup: TradingView Configuration

TradingView is your eyes. We need to strip away the noise.

Step-by-Step Configuration:

  • Clean the Chart: Remove the grid background (Set to 'None'). It distracts the eye.
  • Candlestick Colors: Set increasing candles to Green (#089981) and decreasing to Red (#F23645). Make borders and wicks the same color.
  • Timeframes: Add these to your favorites (Star icon): 5m, 15m, 1h, Daily.
    5m: For Entry/Exit | 15m: For Trend | 1h/Daily: For Big Levels
  • Watchlist: Create a new watchlist named 'Intraday Focus'. Add: NIFTY 50, BANK NIFTY, RELIANCE, HDFCBANK, ICICIBANK, TCS, INFY.

üí° Pro Tip: Dark Mode

Always use Dark Mode. You will be staring at screens for hours. White backgrounds cause eye strain and fatigue, leading to bad decisions.

üöÄ 2. Execution Setup: Broker Terminal

Your broker (Zerodha/Upstox/etc.) is your trigger. It must be set for instant execution.

Essential Settings:

  • Market Watch: Mirror your TradingView watchlist here. Don't waste time searching for symbols during market hours.
  • One-Click/Instant Order: If your broker supports it, enable it. It bypasses the order confirmation popup. (Warning: Be careful!)
  • Chart Pop-out: Learn to pop out the order window or chart. If you have two screens, put execution on one and charts on the other.
  • Funds Check: Ensure your account is funded the night before. Don't scramble to add money at 9:15 AM.

üîé 3. Selection Setup: The Pre-Market Screener

How do you find stocks to trade? You cannot watch 2000 stocks. You need a screener.

We will use a simple, free screener like Chartink.

The "Gap Up/Down" Logic:

We want stocks that are opening with momentum. Here is a basic Logic for 9:15 AM:

OPEN > PREV DAY HIGH (Gap Up)
OR
OPEN < PREV DAY LOW (Gap Down)

Bookmark a screener page that shows "Top Gainers" and "Top Losers" for the first 15 mins.

  3. Log into your Broker and find the "Order Settings". Familiarize yourself with "Limit", "Market", and "Stop Loss-Market (SL-M)" order types. Do not place a trade yet.
