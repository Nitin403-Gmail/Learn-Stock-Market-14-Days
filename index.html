The 7 Pillars of Long-Term Investing

Master these core concepts to build a solid foundation for your investing journey:

1

The Investor's Mindset

Understanding that you are buying a piece of a business, not just a ticker symbol. Embracing a long-term outlook.

2

Fundamental Analysis

How to analyze a company's financial health, competitive advantages (moat), and management quality.

3

Valuation Basics

Learning how to estimate a company's intrinsic value. "Price is what you pay, value is what you get."

4

Circle of Competence

The importance of investing only in businesses you understand. Defining the boundaries of your knowledge.

5

Portfolio Construction

How to build a diversified portfolio of great companies and avoid putting all your eggs in one basket.

6

Behavioral Psychology

Recognizing and overcoming common psychological biases that lead to poor investment decisions.

7

The Power of Compounding

Understanding how consistent, long-term investment allows your wealth to grow exponentially over time.
